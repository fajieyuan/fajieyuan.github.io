I am a Ph.D. student supervised by Professor Joemon Jose in the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow. I graduated from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). in 2013. Before joining Glasgow University in Oct. 2014, I had worked as an Android engineer in Cloud Computing Center, Chinese Academy of Science (Dongguan) for one year. My Ph.D. project is supported by Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) .

Research Interests

My research interests include Recommender System, Machine Learning, L2R, Muiti-Doc Summarization, Information Retrieval.
