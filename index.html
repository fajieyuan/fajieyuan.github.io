I am a Ph.D. student supervised by Professor Joemon Jose in the School of Computing Science at the University of Glasgow since Oct. 2014. Prior to that, I graduated from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2013. From 2013-2014, I had worked as an Android engineer in Cloud Computing Center, Chinese Academy of Science (Dongguan) for one year. My Ph.D. project is supported by Chinese Government Scholarship (CSC) . My research interests span recommender system (RS), machine learning (ML), data mining (DM).
